## ✨ Why NutriChef?

NutriChef is a modern **AI Recipe SaaS Platform** designed to solve a real-world problem:

❌ Confused what to cook  
❌ Limited ingredients at home  
❌ No personalized meal ideas  

✅ NutriChef gives instant smart recipes in seconds.

Built as a **professional full-stack project** to showcase industry-ready skills and product thinking. Well-presented READMEs often create a stronger first impression for repositories. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
